How to work with me

I value sincerity and transparency in my coworkers. I enjoy frequent exchanges on how to improve our work together. I appreciate constructive criticism as there is always room for improvement. I expect my coworkers to be open to suggestions as well.

In a team, I seek a collaborative group. I want to be on the same wavelength with everyone, and I particularly appreciate being updated on the progress of each member's work, so we can all intervene together if problems arise.

Contact me via email (response time: 1 day) or Telegram (response time: 1 hour). For long and detailed messages (which I prefer), email is better.

When joining an established group or starting a new project, I prefer to have some time for independent research first. Then, I like to hear the opinions of those with experience in the field or who have previously worked on the project. After these steps, I work very independently.
