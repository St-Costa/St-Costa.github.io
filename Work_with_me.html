I work my best when...

  • → Project is well-managed, with clear goals and a solid plan to achieve them
  • → Good collaboration among team members, with ideas being bounced around
  • → Communication is clear and sincere, including constructive (even harsh) critique
  • → Updates are well-timed, neither too frequent nor completely absent
  • → Healthy work-life balance

How to give me feedback

  • → Directly, but in a constructive manner
  • → In a relaxed moment (i.e. not when I am stressed), as it allows for better processing
  • → With a clear rationalization and reasoning behind it, rather than simply being told 'you should do X.' Understanding the 'why' behind helps me see the bigger picture and implement

I am stressed when...

  • → I don't communicate (effectively or at all)
  • → I have a sad / angry resting face
  • → I am unproductive
